About Kannur

Kannur, also known as Cannanore, is a coastal city in Kerala, India. It is famous for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and serene beaches.

Discover the rich history, art forms, and natural beauty of Kannur!

Popular Attractions

Payyambalam Beach

A pristine beach with golden sands, perfect for swimming and sunset views.

St. Angelo's Fort

A historic fort overlooking the Arabian Sea, offering panoramic views and a glimpse into the past.

Muzhappilangad Beach

The longest drive-in beach in Asia, known for its serene ambiance and adventure sports.
