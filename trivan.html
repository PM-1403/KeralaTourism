About Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala, a state in India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic landmarks, and natural beauty.

Explore the city's attractions, festivals, and more!

Popular Attractions

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A famous Hindu temple known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.

Napier Museum

An art and natural history museum showcasing a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits.

Kuthiramalika Palace

A historic palace featuring intricate wooden carvings, sculptures, and ancient artifacts.
