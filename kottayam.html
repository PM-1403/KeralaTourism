About Kottayam

Kottayam, often referred to as the Land of Letters, is a charming city in Kerala, India. It is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, serene backwaters, and lush greenery.

Explore the vibrant culture and scenic beauty of Kottayam!

Popular Attractions

Kumarakom

A tranquil backwater destination known for its picturesque landscapes and houseboat cruises.

Vembanad Lake

The largest lake in Kerala, offering mesmerizing views and opportunities for boating and birdwatching.

Thazhathangady

Famous for its ancient St. Mary's Church and beautiful backwater scenery.
