About Kollam

Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a port city in the southern state of Kerala, India. It is known for its historic significance, backwaters, and scenic beauty.

Explore the city's rich culture, heritage, and natural attractions!

Popular Attractions

Ashtamudi Lake

A scenic backwater destination famous for its houseboat cruises and tranquil environment.

Thangassery Lighthouse

A historic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and the surrounding area.

Sasthamkotta Lake

The largest freshwater lake in Kerala, known for its scenic beauty and religious significance.
