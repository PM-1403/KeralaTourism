About Ernakulam

Ernakulam, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant city in Kerala, India. It is the commercial and industrial hub of the state, offering a blend of modernity and tradition.

Discover the rich history, bustling markets, and picturesque landscapes of Ernakulam!

Popular Attractions

Fort Kochi

A historic neighborhood known for its architecture, charming streets, and Chinese fishing nets.

Marine Drive

A scenic promenade along the backwaters, perfect for leisurely walks and boat rides.

Hill Palace

A museum complex featuring artifacts, antiques, and exhibits showcasing Kerala's royal heritage.
