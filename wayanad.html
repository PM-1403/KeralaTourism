About Wayanad

Wayanad, situated amidst the Western Ghats, is renowned for its lush green landscapes, dense forests, and diverse wildlife. It is a paradise for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and wildlife photographers.

Explore the serene beauty and rich biodiversity of Wayanad!

Popular Attractions

Edakkal Caves

Ancient rock formations with petroglyphs dating back to the Neolithic age, offering panoramic views of the surroundings.

Chembra Peak

The highest peak in Wayanad, ideal for trekking and offering breathtaking views of the mist-covered valleys.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

A protected area rich in biodiversity, home to elephants, tigers, leopards, and numerous bird species.
