About Idukki

Idukki is a beautiful hill station located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. It is renowned for its captivating natural beauty, including hills, valleys, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Discover the serenity and tranquility of Idukki!

Popular Attractions

Munnar

A picturesque hill station known for its tea plantations, misty valleys, and breathtaking landscapes.

Thekkady

Famous for its wildlife sanctuary, Thekkady offers opportunities for trekking, boating, and wildlife spotting.

Idukki Dam

One of the largest arch dams in Asia, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and heritage.
